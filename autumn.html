Kinosaki in Autumn

You can ride a ropeway and enjoy the red and orange scenery of autumn leaves while climbing the mountain, and at the top, you can see the whole of Kinosaki. If you enjoy a hot spring tour in a pleasant climate, you will be able to relax mentally and physically. During autumn, there are many festivals you can enjoy such as The Danjiri festival.
