Kinosaki in Summer

You can enjoy visiting "Amarube Railway" and swimming on Takeno beach. Japanese-style summer festivals are held in the hot spring district of Kinosaki, and you can take a walk while browsing night shops and see the fireworks. Why don't you enjoy your summer vacation in Kinosaki, surrounded by the blue sea and sky?
