Hot spring

Hot spting

There are seven hot springs in the Kinosaki Onsen district, and you can enjoy the yumeguri tour of each hot spring wearing yukata.

In addition to being able to enjoy various kinds of water with different efficacy in each hot spring, the scenery from the open-air bath and the foot bath are also key points for enjoying Kinosaki hot spring." Also, Kinosaki Onsen has become a tattoo-free hot spring resort in consideration of the increasing number of foreign tourists.

As one of Japan's hot spring cultures, there are still many hot springs where tattoos are prohibited, but you can spend a relaxing time in Kinosaki without worrying about it.

Kinosaki Marine World
