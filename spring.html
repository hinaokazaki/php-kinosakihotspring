Kinosaki in Spring

In spring, you can enjoy the hot spring resort in pleasant weather with beautiful scenery surrounded by cherry trees in full bloom. You can see various kinds of cherry blossoms, such as illuminated night cherry blossoms and cherry blossom petals floating on the water. Japanese sweets associated with the strawberry season are also popular.
